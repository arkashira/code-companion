# marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
**Primary Positioning Statement:**  
"Code Companion: Your AI-powered guide to navigating the technical landscape of building AI tools, empowering non-technical founders to turn their visions into reality."

**Alternative Positioning Statements:**
1. "Transform your ideas into AI-driven solutions with Code Companion, the ultimate technical partner for non-technical founders."
2. "Unlock your potential as a non-technical builder with Code Companion, bridging the gap between vision and execution in AI development."
3. "Code Companion: The AI knowledge augmentation tool that empowers non-technical founders to confidently build and launch innovative AI products."

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
1. **Persona 1: Sarah the Startup Founder**  
   - **Daily Job:** Managing a small team, pitching to investors, and overseeing product development.  
   - **Budget:** $500/month for tools and resources.

2. **Persona 2: Tom the Tech-Savvy Marketer**  
   - **Daily Job:** Developing marketing strategies, analyzing data, and collaborating with technical teams to launch campaigns.  
   - **Budget:** $300/month for marketing and tech tools.

3. **Persona 3: Lisa the Product Manager**  
   - **Daily Job:** Coordinating between stakeholders, defining product requirements, and ensuring timely delivery of features.  
   - **Budget:** $400/month for project management and development tools.

## Channels
1. **Content Marketing (Blog & SEO)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 per acquired customer through organic traffic and lead generation.

2. **Webinars & Workshops**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $75 per acquired customer through educational sessions that showcase product value.

3. **Social Media Advertising (LinkedIn & Facebook)**  
   - **CAC Estimate:** $100 per acquired customer through targeted ads focusing on non-technical founders.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1:** Launch blog post introducing Code Companion; social media teasers.
- **Week 2:** Publish case study featuring early users; host a webinar on overcoming technical barriers.
- **Week 3:** Release a video tutorial on using Code Companion effectively; share user testimonials.
- **Week 4:** Blog post on AI trends for non-technical founders; engage in discussions on relevant forums.
- **Week 5:** Launch a newsletter featuring tips for non-technical builders; promote upcoming webinar.
- **Week 6:** Publish an infographic on the benefits of AI tools for startups; share on social media.
- **Week 7:** Host a Q&A session on social media; gather feedback from users.
- **Week 8:** Release a whitepaper on the importance of technical knowledge in AI development; promote through email marketing.
- **Week 9:** Blog post on common pitfalls for non-technical founders; share insights on social media.
- **Week 10:** Launch a referral program; encourage existing users to share with their networks.
- **Week 11:** Publish a success story of a user who built an AI tool using Code Companion; promote through all channels.
- **Week 12:** Recap of the journey so far; share metrics and user feedback; announce upcoming features.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features; complete beta testing; prepare marketing materials.
- **D-0:** Official launch of Code Companion; initiate marketing campaigns; host launch webinar.
- **D+30:** Collect user feedback; analyze initial metrics; adjust marketing strategies as needed.
- **D+90:** Evaluate product performance; set new goals based on user engagement and feedback; plan for feature updates.

## Success Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** Target of 1,000 DAU by D+90.
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue ($MRR):** Target of $10,000 by D+90.
