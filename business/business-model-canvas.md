# Business Model Canvas for Code Companion

## Customer Segments
- Non-technical founders and builders looking to develop AI-powered tools.
- Startups and small businesses in technology sectors lacking in-house technical expertise.
- Educational institutions and bootcamps teaching AI concepts to non-technical audiences.
- Freelancers and consultants needing to enhance their technical capabilities for client projects.

## Value Propositions
- AI-powered knowledge augmentation that simplifies complex technical concepts for non-technical users.
- Step-by-step guidance and resources tailored to specific AI tool development needs.
- Increased confidence and capability for non-technical users to innovate and build AI solutions.
- Cost-effective alternative to hiring technical co-founders or consultants (estimated savings of $50,000 - $100,000 annually).

## Channels
- Direct sales through the Code Companion website and GitHub repository.
- Online marketing via social media platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter) targeting startup communities.
- Partnerships with educational institutions and tech bootcamps for integration into their curricula.
- Webinars and workshops to demonstrate the product's capabilities and gather user feedback.

## Customer Relationships
- Community-driven support through forums and user groups for peer-to-peer assistance.
- Regular updates and newsletters to keep users informed about new features and best practices.
- Personalized onboarding sessions for new users to maximize product utilization.
- Feedback loops to continuously improve the product based on user experiences and needs.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: Monthly fee of $29 (approx. 1,000 THB) for individual users.
- Tiered pricing for teams and organizations starting at $99/month (approx. 3,500 THB).
- One-time payment for premium features or additional resources (estimated at $199).
- Potential affiliate revenue from partnerships with educational institutions.

## Key Resources
- AI algorithms and models for knowledge augmentation and technical guidance.
- Development team for continuous product improvement and feature expansion.
- Marketing and sales team to drive customer acquisition and engagement.
- Community management resources to foster user engagement and support.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and enhancement of the AI knowledge augmentation tool.
- Marketing campaigns to raise awareness and attract new users.
- User support and community engagement activities to maintain customer satisfaction.
- Data analysis to track user behavior and improve product offerings.

## Key Partners
- Educational institutions and tech bootcamps for curriculum integration and co-marketing.
- AI research organizations for access to cutting-edge technology and insights.
- Influencers and thought leaders in the startup and tech space for promotional partnerships.
- Software development platforms for integration and collaboration opportunities.

## Cost Structure
- Development costs: Salaries for engineers and product managers (estimated at $200,000 annually).
- Marketing expenses: Online advertising, content creation, and community events (approx. $50,000 annually).
- Customer support costs: Salaries for support staff and community managers (estimated at $40,000 annually).
- Operational costs: Hosting, infrastructure, and administrative expenses (approx. $30,000 annually).
