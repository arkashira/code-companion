## Partner Integration Roadmap for Code-Companion

### Target Integrations

| Partner/API                  | Free-Tier Limits                     | Integration Effort | Value-Add (User Job Solved)                                      | Revenue-Share Potential |
|------------------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------|
| **OpenAI**                   | 100,000 tokens/month                 | M                  | Provides advanced AI capabilities for knowledge augmentation.     | Yes                      |
| **Zapier**                   | 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month              | S                  | Automates workflows between apps, enhancing productivity.        | Yes                      |
| **Notion**                   | 1,000 blocks/month                   | M                  | Centralizes project management and documentation for users.      | No                       |
| **Airtable**                 | 1,200 records per base               | M                  | Offers a flexible database solution to manage project data.      | Yes                      |
| **Trello**                   | 10 boards, 250 cards/board           | S                  | Visual project management tool to track progress and tasks.      | No                       |
| **Slack**                    | 5 integrations, 10k messages         | S                  | Facilitates team communication and collaboration.                | Yes                      |
| **Figma**                    | 3 projects, unlimited viewers         | M                  | Enhances design collaboration for product mockups.               | No                       |
| **Google Cloud Platform**    | $300 credit for 90 days              | L                  | Provides scalable cloud infrastructure for AI model deployment.   | Yes                      |

### Rationale for Selection

1. **OpenAI**: As a leader in AI technology, integrating with OpenAI allows Code-Companion to leverage cutting-edge language models, providing users with robust knowledge augmentation capabilities. The revenue-share potential enhances profitability.

2. **Zapier**: This integration allows users to automate repetitive tasks across various applications, significantly improving their productivity and efficiency. The affiliate program can generate additional revenue.

3. **Notion**: While Notion does not offer a revenue-share, its integration can enhance user experience by providing a centralized platform for project management and documentation, which is critical for non-technical founders.

4. **Airtable**: This integration will help users manage their project data effectively, allowing for better organization and tracking of tasks. The revenue-share potential makes it a financially attractive option.

5. **Trello**: Although it does not offer a revenue-share, Trello's visual project management capabilities can help users manage their projects more effectively, addressing a key pain point.

6. **Slack**: Integrating with Slack enhances team communication, which is essential for non-technical founders who may struggle with collaboration. The revenue-share potential adds financial incentive.

7. **Figma**: While Figma does not provide a revenue-share, it is essential for design collaboration, allowing users to create and iterate on product designs seamlessly.

8. **Google Cloud Platform**: This integration offers scalable infrastructure for deploying AI models, addressing the technical limitations faced by users. The revenue-share potential can significantly boost profitability.

### Conclusion

The selected integrations not only provide essential tools for non-technical founders but also enhance the overall value proposition of Code-Companion. Prioritizing partnerships with revenue-share potential will help drive profitability while addressing user pain points effectively.
