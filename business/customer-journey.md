# Customer Journey Map for Code-Companion

## Phase 1: Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Non-technical founders/builders encounter challenges while trying to build AI-powered tools.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about available resources; feeling overwhelmed by technical jargon.
- **User Emotions**: Confusion, frustration, and a sense of inadequacy.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Create engaging content (blogs, webinars) that demystifies AI and showcases success stories of non-technical founders using Code-Companion.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and content engagement rates (e.g., blog views, webinar sign-ups).

## Phase 2: Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Founders start researching solutions to their technical knowledge gaps.
- **Friction Points**: Information overload; difficulty in comparing tools; skepticism about AI solutions.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful yet cautious; feeling overwhelmed by choices.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide clear comparison charts, testimonials, and case studies that highlight how Code-Companion has helped others in similar situations.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and inquiries about the product.

## Phase 3: Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a free trial or demo of Code-Companion.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; unclear onboarding process; potential technical issues.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with anxiety; fear of failure.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Offer personalized onboarding sessions, interactive tutorials, and responsive customer support to guide users through their first experience.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement during the trial period.

## Phase 4: Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users successfully integrate Code-Companion into their workflow.
- **Friction Points**: Ongoing technical challenges; need for continuous support; potential feature limitations.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction from overcoming initial hurdles but still feeling uncertain about long-term usage.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular check-ins, advanced training sessions, and community forums to foster engagement and support.
- **Metric**: Retention rate and user satisfaction scores (e.g., NPS).

## Phase 5: Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users recognize the value of Code-Companion and seek to utilize more features or upgrade.
- **Friction Points**: Uncertainty about which additional features to adopt; fear of complexity in advanced features.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in their abilities but cautious about expanding their usage.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Provide tailored recommendations for feature expansion based on user behavior and needs; showcase advanced use cases and success stories.
- **Metric**: Upsell conversion rate and feature adoption rates.
